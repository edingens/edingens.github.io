The Problem

The Environmental Cost of Imported Food

Carbon Footprint

Emissions from transportation, refrigeration, and packaging for imported foods significantly impact the environment.

Resource Use

Mass agriculture for exports contributes to water overuse and deforestation.

Impact on Small Farmers

Global supply chains marginalize local farmers and erode food sovereignty.
